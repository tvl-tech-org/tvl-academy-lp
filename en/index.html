AI Design Sprint

A clear framework to decide where AI automation is actually worth it. We use a single working artifact, the AI Opportunity Card, throughout the sprint, from workflow and pain point to pilot and success criteria.

Working Rule
One workflow. One painful step. One measurable gain. One card.

AI Design Sprint is the practical framework we use to identify, evaluate and prioritize real AI opportunities inside the company. We do not start from tools, but from processes, bottlenecks and observable time loss in day-to-day work.

Define the workflow

Give it a clear name, define the owning department, the trigger and the done state.

Write the flow

Five to eight short steps, in the real order of execution.

Pick the painful step

One single friction point, backed by real examples.

Define input and output

Source system, testable outcome, human approval if needed.

Score and pilot

Prioritize objectively and move into a pilot with metrics and guardrails.

Prioritization Formula
Score = (Volume × Minutes × Impact × Feasibility) ÷ Risk

We use the same formula for every card so prioritization stays consistent across teams, processes and departments.

Volume How many times the workflow appears in a week.
Minutes How much time is lost on each execution.
Impact & Feasibility How much it matters and how easy it is to pilot.
Risk How costly an error would be if the output is wrong.
