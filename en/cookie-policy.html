Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and local storage

On this landing page we use a minimal set of mechanisms for cookie preferences, analytics and the external contact form. Analytics starts only after consent, while the Pipedrive form is embedded directly on the page.

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. What we actually use on the site

Mechanism Provider Purpose When it appears Duration
tvl_cookie_consent_v1 in local storage academy.tvl.tech Stores your Google Analytics choice. After you choose Accept or Decline in the banner. 180 days
_ga and _ga_* cookies Google Analytics 4 Measures traffic and interaction with the page. Only after consent for analytics. According to Google Analytics settings
__cf_bm Pipedrive / Cloudflare Anti-bot protection for the external form. When the page loads together with the embedded Pipedrive form. About 1 hour
_GRECAPTCHA Google reCAPTCHA Anti-spam risk analysis for the form. When the page loads, if anti-spam protection is enabled in Pipedrive. According to the provider configuration

2. What we do not do

  • We do not activate Google Analytics before consent.
  • We do not use Google advertising signals or ad personalization in this analytics setup.
  • We do not use Google advertising cookies in this implementation.

3. How you can change your choice

  • On the main page, you can use the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer to reopen the banner and change your analytics preference.
  • If you decline analytics, we also attempt to clear the existing Google Analytics cookies from the current browser.
  • The banner preference controls only Google Analytics. The Pipedrive form embedded on the page is not turned on or off by this setting.
  • You can also clear cookies from your browser settings.

4. Additional information

For details about the processing of personal data, see our Privacy Policy. For questions, you can write to us at sales@tvl.tech.

The Pipedrive form is an external integration displayed directly on the page. In practical terms, the resources and technologies associated with that provider may be loaded as soon as the page is displayed.
